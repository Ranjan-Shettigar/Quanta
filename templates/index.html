About Quanta

Quanta is a revolutionary AI-powered chat application built on the Google Gemini API. Our user-friendly interface is crafted with the expertise of Claude.ai 3.5 Sonnet, and the backend is powered by OpenAI's ChatGPT 4o.

Key Features:

  • Advanced AI-driven conversations
  • Intuitive and engaging user interface
  • Seamless integration across various knowledge domains
  • Continuous learning and improvement
